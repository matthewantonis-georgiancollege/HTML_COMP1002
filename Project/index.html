Who We Are

Nintendo is a leading entertainment company that has been entertaining families and bringing joy to the world for over a century.In 1985, the company introduced the Nintendo Entertainment System, a home video game console that has revolutionized the way people play video games. With its innovative design and groundbreaking game play, the NES has quickly become a must-have for families and gamers alike. At Nintendo, the belief is that video games should be enjoyable for everyone, regardless of age or skill level. This is why the company strives to create games that are not only entertaining, but also easy to pick up and play. From classic franchises to new and exciting IPs, Nintendo is dedicated to delivering the highest quality gaming experiences. The company continues to push the boundaries of what is possible in the world of video games and is committed to bringing joy and excitement to players everywhere. Join the Nintendo family and experience the magic of gaming like never before.
